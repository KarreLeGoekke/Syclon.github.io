Lore:

You are one of many security guards that work at pandora industries, which was abandoned in one reality where the US Government took control over all laboratories in their country. Your mission is to go into those abandoned laboratories, exterminate anomalous beings, and kill anyone that interferes with you and your friends.

Description:

This is a spinoff of a map that is currently being processed, which is dr_pandoralab (codenamed Labrat). But unlike dr_pandoralab, you are not in a deathrun where you have to go through traps that are being activated by death. Instead, you have to survive by looting corpses and killing alien beings, as well as human forces, most specifically the Feds.
